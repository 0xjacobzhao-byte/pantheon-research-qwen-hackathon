# Commercialization Plan

How Pantheon Research intends to become a sustainable product. This document
describes **expected revenue streams and sequencing** — it makes **no claim of
existing revenue, users, AUM, returns, or pricing.** Where a number is not
verified, the *mechanism* is described instead.

## Principles

- **Recurring first.** Software and research subscriptions before any
  performance-linked economics.
- **Sell governance, not tips.** The product is disciplined, explainable research
  — evidence packs, model comparison, data-quality states — not signals sold as
  guarantees.
- **Trading upside is earned, not assumed.** It is the highest-upside but
  latest-stage pillar, gated behind validation.
- **Never overclaim.** No revenue, user, or performance figure is published until
  it is real and verifiable.

## Revenue architecture (nearest-term → latest-stage)

| # | Pillar | Horizon | Primary mechanism |
|---|--------|---------|-------------------|
| 1 | Subscription research platform | Near | Recurring subscription |
| 2 | Investment skills marketplace | Near–Mid | Per-skill / bundle / license |
| 3 | Paid equity-evaluation & data APIs | Mid | Usage / per-report / enterprise |
| 4 | B2B / institutional research licensing | Mid | Annual license + services |
| 5 | Long-term proprietary strategy upside | Late | Performance-linked (future, gated) |

### 1. Subscription research platform
The cross-asset dashboard (macro regime, equity research, BTC / ETH / DeFi, FICC,
technical analysis, Research Ops), AI research overlays, model comparison, and
research briefs — sold as tiered monthly / annual subscriptions with team seats.
*Membership and payment foundations are developed; pricing and GTM validation are
in progress. No pricing is published here.*

### 2. Investment skills marketplace
Pantheon's deterministic frameworks packaged as **reusable, versioned investment
skills** — Global Macro, US / China / Hong Kong / Singapore Equity, Bitcoin,
Ethereum, DeFi Yield, Technical Analysis, FX, Commodities — sold per-skill, as
bundles, or via enterprise/white-label licensing. A skill packages framework
logic, data requirements, output schema, risk gates, and update cadence. **A
skill is not a guarantee of returns and not an autonomous trading strategy.**

### 3. Paid equity-evaluation & data APIs
- **Equity Evaluation API** — structured company quality, moat, cash engine,
  valuation, risk summary, evidence pack, LLM comparison, missing evidence,
  confidence, and data-quality state.
- **Data & Evidence API** — canonical observations, normalized snapshots,
  evidence artifacts, provider health, provenance, and quality labels.

Sold usage-based, per-report, or as an enterprise tier.

### 4. B2B / institutional research licensing
Annual licensing of governed research surfaces and model-comparison outputs to
desks and platforms that need explainable, auditable research infrastructure
rather than raw signals — bundled with integration services.

### 5. Long-term proprietary strategy upside (gated)
The highest-upside but **latest-stage** pillar. Any performance-linked economics
becomes credible **only after**:

1. point-in-time **backtesting** across the deterministic frameworks;
2. a validated **forward track record** (no-lookahead, published discipline);
3. an **approval workflow** with a staged Trading Gateway (shadow → paper →
   approval) that stays fail-closed; and
4. explicit **risk controls**.

**Autonomous trading is not a current product**, and **no trading profit is
claimed today.** See [`validation_methodology.md`](validation_methodology.md) and
[`roadmap`](../README.md#18-commercialization-and-roadmap).

## Moat

- **Governance and provenance** — evidence packs, `sha256` hashing, fail-closed
  data states, and model comparison are hard to replicate credibly.
- **Workflow lock-in** — value compounds as Pantheon becomes part of a research
  team's daily workflow.
- **Multi-model, multi-cloud posture** — no single model or cloud vendor is a
  permanent architectural dependency; Qwen on Alibaba Cloud is a first-class
  Asia-facing path.

## What is explicitly not claimed

- No existing revenue, paying users, AUM, or conversion figures.
- No alpha / return / Sharpe / hit-rate performance before forward validation.
- No autonomous trading and no live order path in this public repo.
